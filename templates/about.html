About ApnaParivar

ApnaParivar is a secure, private space for you to store and share your family's precious moments. Create your own private gallery and generate time-limited, secure access codes for family members to view your albums.

Our goal is to provide a simple, elegant, and private alternative to public social media for your most personal photos.

How it Works

  1. Sign Up: Create a new account. Your account will be "pending" review.
  2. Subscribe: Make the payment as per the instructions in the "Subscribe" button.
  3. Email Us: Send your payment reference ID to `youremail@gmail.com`.
  4. Get Authorized: Our Super Admin will verify your payment and authorize your account.
  5. Create: Once authorized, you can log in to your Admin Panel to upload images and create your private gallery.
  6. Share: Generate a unique, time-limited access code to share with your family and friends.
